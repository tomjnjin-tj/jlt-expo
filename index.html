Common Q&A
MOQ? — Typically 500–1,000 linear meters for standard profiles. Custom tooling required for new cross-sections.

Lead time? — Samples: 3–5 weeks. Production: 8–12 weeks after tooling approval.

Tooling cost? — Amortized into unit price or charged separately depending on volume commitment.

IATF 16949? — Yes, certified at both Mexico and China plants.

EV seals? — Yes. Battery-grade silicone, thermal profiles, low-NVH door seals, lightweight foam composites all available.

Can you visit our factory? — Yes, we welcome factory visits. Contact us to arrange.
